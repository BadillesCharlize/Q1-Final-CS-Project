"A Decaying World"

Climate change has been a consistent issue throughout the 20th century, yet seemingly little progress has been done to combat this problem. Due to this, the climate around the world has significantly been more extreme, especially here in the Philippines with consistent forty degree weather. That being said, significant changes have been occurring behind the scenes, and if we act more proactively we will be able to curb this issue in the near future.
