4.6 billion years ago...

The earth was being formed. A ball of molten rock and lava! How could anyone imagine this planet would eventually become habitable? Well, some asteroids filled with tiny bits of water fell onto the surface of the earth, and eventually...

4.0 billion years ago...

Who knew! That water was a key ingredient to creating life! Isn't that so wonderful? Life came from such extreme conditions, and yet! It managed to survive and thrive, creating an ecosystem that still defines our lives till this day.

547 million years ago...

Well look at what we have now! Plants have finally started flourishing! The world looks a lot more green now, and the continents have started to form! (Though they didn't look the same as today.)

201 million years ago...

We finally get to meet our beloved friends, the iconic dinosaurs. In this time period they started to fluorish around the globe. It's at this point where the continents have started to take more similar forms as seen today. But not everything good lasts forever...
